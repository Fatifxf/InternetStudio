The Meadow

Edward in the sunlight was shocking. His skin sparkled, unnatural and mesmerizing.
Bella stopped in her tracks, watching as Edward disappeared into the trees. Her breath caught in her throat, but she didn’t follow. The wind howled through the clearing, carrying away any lingering warmth. He was gone. She wrapped her arms around herself, the absence of his presence colder than the night air. Maybe this was for the best. Maybe some things weren’t meant to be.

Turn away and leave it all behind. | Regret lingers.
Or was it proof of something more extraordinary?
