The Meadow

Edward in the sunlight was shocking. His skin sparkled, unnatural and mesmerizing.
Bella hesitated. Could something so beautiful still be monstrous?
Bella's chest ached with conflicting emotions. She wanted him close, but fear gnawed at the edges of her resolve. Edward’s golden gaze held her in place. "You hesitate," he murmured. "Why?" Because this was dangerous. Because the closer they got, the more impossible it became to ignore the risk. And yet, she stayed.
