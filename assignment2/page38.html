The Meadow

Edward in the sunlight was shocking. His skin sparkled, unnatural and mesmerizing.
Edward shut his eyes. A tremor ran through him. Then, suddenly, he was gone. Bella’s breath hitched. She turned in time to see him in the shadows, gripping his own arms like a man at war with himself. "I need to go," he rasped.
